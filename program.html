Workshop Program

Overview

This year Cloud@MICRO workshop will be held as a fully online global event in conjunction with MICRO 2021 - 54rd IEEE/ACM International Symposium on Microarchitecture®. The presentations for this workshop will be recorded and available to all MICRO 2021 conference attendees.

Pleae note the timezone for this workshop is EDT (UTC-4).

Registration

MICRO 2021 conference registration gives the access to all symposium sessions, all workshops/tutorials, and all recordings that are made as part of MICRO 2021. Please check the registration information page for more details.
